Confused on how to use Wealth Concierge?

Start with entering your age into the demo input. From there you need to click create yours.

Once you fill out the very long and thorough signup form, scroll down to portfolio.

If it is your first time using the app then you will need to take the risk score. It is 16 questions and think hard and answer as truthfully as you can.

Once you have completed the risk score, all you need to do is hit portfolio button again and there you are! Your own custom portfolio

There are bonus features like housing which will give you a trend of the last 12 months of housing data for your zipcode.

The dashboard button will take you to a new page and you can begin to buy and track your performance of your portfolio.
